This document is the record of all the changes in [**the Reports API**](/data-reporting/reports-api/) starting from **version 3.2**.

## [v3.4] - Developer preview

- POST method becomes primary and recommended way of sending requests. GET method is still supported, however due to limitations of query string length might fail for more complex requests.
- Request format has a new format, the filters are nested within the `filters` field now.
- To provide more consistent experience across Platform APIs, filters were reworked and aligned with those used in the **List Archives** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.4/#list-archives). All reports:
  - had `from` field renamed to `filters.from`
  - had `to` field renamed to `filters.to`
  - had `groups` field renamed to `filters.groups.values`
  - had `agents` field renamed to `filters.agents.values`
  - had `agent_assigned` field renamed to `filters.agents.exists`
  - had `names` field renamed to `filters.tags.values`
  - had `names:with` field renamed to `filters.tags.exists`
  - had `names:without` field renamed to `filters.tags.exists`
  - had `tags` field renamed to `filters.tags.values`
  - had `tagged ` field renamed to `filters.tags.exists`
  - had `customer_client_ids` field renamed to `filters.properties.source.customer_client_id.values`
  - have a new filters: `filters.agents.exclude_values`, `filters.agents.exists`, `filters.tags.exclude_values`, `filters.properties.<namespace>.<name>.values`, `filters.properties.<namespace>.<name>.exclude_values`, `filters.properties.<namespace>.<name>.exists`, `filters.sales.values`, `filters.sales.exclude_values`, `filters.sales.exists`, `filters.goals.values`, `filters.goals.exclude_values`, `filters.goals.exists`, `filters.surveys.type`, `filters.surveys.answer_id`, `filters.events.types`.

## [v3.3] - 2021-03-30

### Chats

- There's a new request property, timezone (e.g. America/Phoenix), for the [**Agents Chatting Duration**](/data-reporting/reports-api/v3.3/#agents-chatting-duration) and [**Tags**](/data-reporting/reports-api/v3.3/#tags) reports.
- There's a new method, [**Total Chats**](/data-reporting/reports-api/v3.3/#total_chats), which shows how many chats occurred during the specified period.

## [v3.2] - 2021-01-07

### Chats

- There's a new method, [**Agents Chatting Duration**](/data-reporting/reports-api/v3.2/#agents-chatting-duration), which shows the average chatting duration for agents.
- There's a new method, [**Tags**](/data-reporting/reports-api/v3.2/#tags), which shows the distribution of tags for chats.
