This document is the record of all the changes in [**the Reports API**](/data-reporting/reports-api/) starting from **version 3.2**.

## [v3.4] - Developer preview

No changes yet.

## [v3.3] - 2021-03-30

### Chats

- There's a new request property, timezone (e.g. America/Phoenix), for the [**Agents Chatting Duration**](/data-reporting/reports-api/v3.3/#agents-chatting-duration) and [**Tags**](/data-reporting/reports-api/v3.3/#tags) reports.
- There's a new method, [**Total Chats**](/data-reporting/reports-api/v3.3/#total_chats), which shows how many chats occurred during the specified period.

## [v3.2] - 2021-01-07

### Chats

- There's a new method, [**Agents Chatting Duration**](/data-reporting/reports-api/v3.2/#agents-chatting-duration), which shows the average chatting duration for agents.
- There's a new method, [**Tags**](/data-reporting/reports-api/v3.2/#tags), which shows the distribution of tags for chats.
