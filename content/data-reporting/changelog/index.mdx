This document is the record of all the changes in [**the Reports API**](/data-reporting/reports-api/) starting from **version 3.2**.

## [v3.3] - Developer preview
## [v3.2] - 2021-01-16

### Added

- There's a new method, [**Agents Chatting Duration**](/data-reporting/reports-api/#agents-chatting-duration), which shows the average chatting duration for agents.
- The chat-related methods ([**Total Chats**](/data-reporting/reports-api/#total-chats), [**Chat Engagement**]((/data-reporting/reports-api/#chat-engagement)), [**Chat Ratings Report**](/data-reporting/reports-api/#chat-ratings-report), [**Chat Ranking**](/data-reporting/reports-api/#chat-ranking)) have a new optional parameter, `tags`, to return the statistics for a specific tag.
