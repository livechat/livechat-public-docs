This document is the record of all the changes in the **Agent Chat API**, Web and RTM references, starting from **version 3.0**.

## [v3.2] - 2020-06-18

### Chats

- The **Get Archives** method was renamed to **List Archives**.
- The **List Archives** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#list-archives) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#list-archives)) method has a new filter, `agents`. It replaces the `agents_ids` filter.
- The **List Archives** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#list-archives) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#list-archives)) method has a new filter, `events.types`.
- The **List Archives** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#list-archives) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#list-archives)) method has new pagination with hashed page IDs replacing the old pagination format. Pagination is now uniform across the whole Agent Chat API.
- The **List Archives** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#list-archives) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#list-archives)) method has a new parameter, `highlights`. It allows you to highlight the match of `filters.query` with an HTML tag.
- The **Get Chats Summary** method was renamed to **List Chats**.
- The  **List Chats** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#list-chats) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#list-chats)) method has a new filter, `include_chats_without_threads`.
- In **List Chats** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#list-chats) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#list-chats)), the `order` field was renamed to `sort_order` .
- In **List Chats** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#list-chats) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#list-chats)), the `order` field in the `chat` object was removed from the method response.
- There's a new method **List Threads** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#list-threads) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#list-threads)).
- The **Get Chat Threads** method evolved into **Get Chat** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#get-chat) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#get-chat)). It now allows to retrieve a chat containing a particular thread. It also changed its shape; it no longer contains the `order` field and the `chat` key.
- The **Close Thread** method was renamed to **Deactivate Chat**. The corresponding push, **thread_closed**, was renamed to **chat_deactivated**.
- The `incoming_chat_thread` push was renamed to `incoming_chat`.
- The [**incoming_chat**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#incoming_chat) push has new fields, `requester_id` and `transferred_from`.
- The **Get Chat Threads Summary** method was removed.
- There's a new push, [**chat_unfollowed**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#chat_unfollowed), complementary to the **Unfollow Chat** method.

### Chat access

- The **Chat access** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#chat-access) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#chat-access)) methods, `grant_access` and `revoke_access` were renamed, respectively, to `grant_chat_access` and `revoke_chat_access` along with the corresponding [pushes](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#chat-access-1). These methods no longer accept the `resource` parameter.
- The **Set Chat Access** method was removed. Its use cases are now covered by the **Transfer Chat**.
- **Transfer Chat** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#transfer-chat) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#transfer-chat)) can now be used with inactive chats.
- The **chat_access_set** push was removed.
- The [**chat_transferred**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#chat_transferred-1) push is no longer tied directly to **Transfer Chat**. Now, it can be generated when the transfer is implicit, for example, agents are reassigned due to their inactivity.
- There are new fields, `requester_id`, `thread_id`, `reason`, `queue` and `transferred_to` in the **chat_transferred** push.
- The `type` and `ids` fields were removed from the **chat_transferred** push.

### Chat users

- The **Add User to Chat** method ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#add-user-to-chat) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#add-user-to-chat)) accepts an optional parameter `require_active_thread`.
- The **chat_user_added** push was renamed to [**user_added_to_chat**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#user_added_to_chat). It has new fields: `reason` and `requester_id`.
- The **chat_user_removed** push was renamed to [**user_removed_from_chat**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#user_removed_from_chat). It has new fields: `reason` and `requester_id`.
- The `user_type` field was removed from the **user_added_to_chat** and **user_removed_from_chat** pushes.

### Events

- There's a new parameter `id` in the [**Form field**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#form-fields) data structure (`fields.answer.id` for the `group_chooser` field type).  It's an identifier for each option Customers can choose.
- There are new fields, `previous_thread_id`, `next_thread_id`, `queue` and `queues_duration` in the [**Thread**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#threads) data structure.
- The `order` and `timestamp` fields in the [**Thread**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#threads) data structure were replaced with the new field, `created_at` (date & time in microseconds in UTC).
- There's a new field `queue` in [**Chat summary**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#chat-summaries), nested in `last_thread_summary`.

### Properties

- The [**Property**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#properties) data structure now has the same format in requests as in responses and pushes.
- The **Update Chat Thread Properties** method was renamed to **Update Thread Properties**. The corresponding push, **chat_thread_properties_updated**, was renamed to **thread_properties_updated**.
- The **Delete Chat Thread Properties** method was renamed to **Delete Thread Properties**. The corresponding push, **chat_thread_properties_deleted**, was renamed to **thread_properties_deleted**.

### Thread tags

- The **Tag Chat Thread** method was renamed to **Tag Thread**. The corresponding push, **chat_thread_tagged**, was renamed to **thread_tagged**.
- The **Untag Chat Thread** method was renamed to **Untag Thread**. The corresponding push, **chat_thread_untagged**, was renamed to **thread_untagged**.

### Customers

- There's a new method **Get Customer** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#get-customer) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#get-customer)) that returns info about a given Customer.
- The method **Update Customer** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#update-customer) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#update-customer)), which used to return the Customer data structure, now has no response payload.
- Each method, response, and push that deals with a variation of customer `fields` had this field renamed to `session_fields` and its format changed. It's now an array that respects the item order.
- The **Get Customers** method was renamed to **List Customers**.
- The `order` field was renamed to `sort_order` in the **List Customers** method.

### Status

- The **Update Agent** method was replaced with **Set Routing Status** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#set-routing-status) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#set-routing-status)).
- The **agent_updated** push was replaced with [**routing_status_set**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#routing_status_set).
- There's a new parameter `away` in the [**Login**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#login) method. It was introduced to prevent chat assignments after unexpected reconnects.
- The `order` field in the `chat` object was removed from the responses of the **Login** method.

### Other

- There's a new method **List Agents For Transfer** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#list-agents-for-transfer) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#list-agents-for-transfer)).
- There's a new push, [**queue_positions_updated**](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#queue_positions_updated), with updated positions and wait times for queued chats.
- The `scopes` field in **Multicast** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/#multicast) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.2/rtm-reference/#multicast)) was renamed to `recipients`.

## [v3.1] - 2019-09-17

### Added

- **Web API:** The **Send File** method was replaced with **Upload File** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/#upload-file)). The new method should be used along with **Send Event** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/#send-event) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/rtm-reference/#send-event)), **Start Chat** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/#start-chat) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/rtm-reference/#start-chat)), or **Activate Chat** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/#activate-chat) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/rtm-reference/#activate-chat)).

### Changed

- **Web API:** From now on, sending payloads explicitly in the request body is allowed. You no longer have to wrap it up in the `payload` object.
- **Web API:** When using Bot Agents, you need to include the `X-Author-Id` header in requests instead of sending `author_id` in the request body.
- Method **Update Last Seen Timestamp** was transformed into **Mark Events as Seen** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/#mark-events-as-seen) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/rtm-reference/#mark-events-as-seen)). Now, it accepts the `seen_up_to` parameter (date & time in microseconds in UTC) instead of `timestamp`. Also, it no longer returns `timestamp` in the response.
- Push `last_seen_timestamp_updated` was transformed into [`events_marked_as_seen`](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/rtm-reference/#events_marked_as_seen) with `seen_up_to` parameter instead of `timestamp`.
- Fields `order` and `timestamp` in events were replaced with the new field, `created_at` (date & time in microseconds in UTC).
- Method **Create Customer** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/#create-customer) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/rtm-reference/#create-customer)) now returns only `customer_id` instead of the whole Customer data structure.
- Method **Send Event** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/#send-event) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/rtm-reference/#send-event)) no longer accepts the `require_active_thread` parameter. Thread is always required to be active unless `attach_to_last_thread` is enabled.
- Method **Send Event** now returns only `event_id` instead of the whole [Event data structure](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/#events).
- Method **Start Chat** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/#start-chat) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/rtm-reference/#start-chat)) now returns only `chat_id`, `thread_id` and optionally `event_ids` if requested. It used to return the whole Chat data structure.
- Method **Activate Chat** ([Web](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/#activate-chat) & [RTM](/messaging/agent-chat-api/v3.1/rtm-reference/#activate-chat)) now returns only `thread_id` and optionally `event_ids` if requested. It used to return the whole Chat data structure.

### Removed

- The **Send File** method was removed.
